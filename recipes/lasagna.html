Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets. These noodles are typically boiled, then layered with various fillings. The most well-known version features rich meat sauce (ragù), creamy béchamel, and Parmigiano Reggiano cheese. However, regional variations exist, such as Neapolitan lasagna with tomato sauce, mozzarella, and meatballs, or Ligurian lasagna with pesto. No matter the variation, lasagna is a comforting and flavorful dish that brings people together. Buon appetito! 🍝
